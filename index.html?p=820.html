ong and ong we go

I’m still pretty new to Melbourne myself and so it’s always fun to be a tourist. Although it’s clear that my idea of fun is pretty twisted, but last week I was shown a taste of what’s good in Melbourne - And so commenced one of the best times I’ve had in Melbourne. South American food in Fitzroy, local ales, ciders, meat pies, Mornington wines, Sorrento vanilla slice, amazing Tasmanian produce and cooking, chocolates, french pastries and coffees, coffees, coffees. We went north to the Yarra Ranges, south to the Mornington Peninsula and wandered in the city. So awesome to explore, try new things.

Come visit some time.
