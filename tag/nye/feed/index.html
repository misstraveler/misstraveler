back in the tropics of KL and Singapore, it was tremendous to catch up with friends and family. Headed down to Singapore to check out the sights, count down to the new year and get my flight back to Australia. Also the last time I was there I didn’t check out the Gardens by the Bay, Marina Sands and Universal Studios. As far as weekend trips go – this was a blast. Singapore sure knows how to build ‘em, everything was just super-sized. And another fantastic roller coaster at Universal Studios. A lovely local brought me to live music bar in Holland Village, with European beers, chicken wings and pizza. And the New Year countdown and the view was something to remember for sure.
Here’s wishing you a great 2013!
